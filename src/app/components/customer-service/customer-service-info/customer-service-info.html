Heart Beauty Customer Service

Effective Date: 11 June 2026

1. Customer Service Overview

Heart Beauty is committed to providing a smooth and reliable shopping experience for all customers purchasing our hair products. We aim to ensure that every customer receives clear guidance, timely assistance, and professional support throughout the ordering process.

Our customer service covers assistance with product selection, order placement, payment confirmation, delivery updates, and general enquiries.

All queries are handled in a structured and fair manner to ensure customer satisfaction.

2. How To Buy Hair Online

Shopping with Heart Beauty is designed to be simple and efficient. Please follow the steps below to place your order:

Step 1

Browse Products

Customers may browse our available range of hair products, including wigs, bundles, closures, and frontals.

Step 2

Select Items

Choose your preferred advertised hair product/item based on - hair texture, length, colour, and style. Add the selected items to your cart.

Step 3

Review Cart

Before proceeding to checkout, carefully review all selected items to ensure accuracy in quantity, pricing, and specifications.

Step 4

Checkout and Order Creation

When you proceed to checkout, an order summary will be generated. This includes items selected, quantities, subtotal, any applicable discounts or loyalty points, and the final total. At this stage, no payment is processed yet.

Step 6

Order Confirmation

As an online payment gateway has not yet been implemented, customers complete their order by selecting either "Proceed to Checkout (WhatsApp)" or "Proceed to Checkout (Email)". The selected option automatically generates an order confirmation containing the order details: item/s ordered, item/s properties (color, size etc). This information is sent to Heart Beauty, where our staff review the order, confirm the details with the customer, provide payment instructions, and proceed with order fulfilment.

Step 7

Order Confirmation and Payment Instructions

Once the order is received via WhatsApp or Email, Heart Beauty will confirm product availability, verify pricing and discounts, and provide payment instructions. Customers should not make payment until the order has been confirmed.

Step 8

Payment and Verification

After payment is made, customers must send proof of payment via WhatsApp or Email. Once verified, the order will be processed for dispatch.

Step 9

Order Processing and Delivery

Once payment is confirmed, the order will be prepared and shipped. Delivery updates will be provided where applicable.

3. Payment Process

Heart Beauty operates a manual payment confirmation system. Payments are only accepted after order confirmation.

Orders are not considered complete until:

  • Availability is confirmed
  • Payment has been received
  • Proof of payment has been verified

Failure to follow this process may result in delays or order cancellation.

4. Loyalty Membership System

Heart Beauty offers a loyalty membership system to reward customers.

  • Customers can register as Heart Beauty Members to receive exclusive member-only benefits.
  • Members receive personalised discounts and special offers.
  • Member discounts are sent directly via email and/or WhatsApp based on customer activity and preferences.
  • These offers may include private sales, early access promotions, and curated product deals.
  • Member-only discounts are applied during checkout when available and are subject to specific offer terms.
  • Membership is free and linked to the customer’s Heart Beauty profile upon registration.
  • Heart Beauty reserves the right to modify or withdraw promotional member offers at any time.
